Privacy:

The privacy of the users of our website is our top priority. Windsya aims to inform the users of how their data is being collected, stored and used, to ensure transparency. The privacy policy entails details of the type of data collected and the context behind the collection of data.

If you have any questions or queries about our Privacy Policy, please contact us through our email. We believe in informing our users on what type of data we collect about them, their personal information that they have the right to protect.

Windsya is a twitch clip downloader which collects the basic personal information of the users when they visit the website, automatically agreeing and consenting to the terms of our Privacy Policy. According to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we are your data controller, which means we can legally process and determine the purposes and means of the personal data collected.

The log files that we collect when a user visits the site contain the IP addresses, browser type, the date and time stamp, number of clicks and the Internet Service Provider information which does not personally identify the user. This information helps us in identifying the trends, gather demographic information and track users’ movements to better develop our website.

Windsya uses cookies to store basic information about the users’ preferences and activity which is used as a guideline to optimize the content for them the next time they visit our page.

All our Privacy Policies are applicable only to our online activities and valid for the users of our site in reference to the information that they shared or was collected by Windsya.
